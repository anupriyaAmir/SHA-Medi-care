PURPOSE

This online web application providing information on health, disease, lifestyle and other medicine related topics like Ayurveda, Siddha and Homeopathy. Our website’s aim is to provide members of the public with up-to-date information on health related topic.It helps the people to gain knowledge about various medicines and they can get suggestions from doctors also.

This project is to analyze the name of diseases for other medicines. By analyzing about the type of diseases we will recommend the people to take Ayurveda, Siddha, Homeopathy medicines. From our recommendation and doctor’s suggestion user will get useful tips.
