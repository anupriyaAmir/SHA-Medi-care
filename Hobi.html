Homeopathy medicines for safe weight loss


The homeopathic treatment for losing weight is constitutional. It requires a detailed case history of the patient and its evaluation for selecting a suitable constitutional remedy. Some of the effective homeopathic medicines to lose weight are:
• Calcarea Carbonica
It is mainly prescribed when there is excess fat in the abdomen and the metabolism is working at a very low pace.
• Natrum Mur
It is recommended when there is an excess of fat mainly in the thighs and buttocks as compared to other parts of the body.
• Lycopodium
The patients who require Lycopodium are chronic sufferers of gastric troubles like flatulence and constipation.
• Nux Vomica
It is prescribed for persons who have gained excess weight due to sedentary habits.
• Antimonium Crudum
This medicine mainly prescribed for obese children.
As usual, any homeopathic medication should be used only under the supervision of a certified homeopathic practitioner.
